Our Profile

About the Company

University of Jakarta International (UNIJI) is a step forward taken by Jakarta International College in its commitment to bringing quality education to all students in Indonesia. Our programs are designed to prepare you for your future, be it in building your career or starting your own business.

Vision

UNIJI has a vision to become a leading learning institution capable of achieving an international reputation, which encourages innovation and collaboration in the development of science for the advancement of society.

Mission

From the above vision, UNIJI developed the UNIJI mission as follows:

  1. Developing an excellent learning institution with an international reputation that is able to demonstrate the quality of a world-class university.
  2. Always improving the quality of education, research and innovation in various fields that are the focus of UNIJI, as stated in each faculty.
  3. Encourage collaboration between academics, industry, and society to create graduates who are able to compete globally, both as intrapreneurs and entrepreneurs.
  4. Able to provide solutions that contribute to social and economic progress.
